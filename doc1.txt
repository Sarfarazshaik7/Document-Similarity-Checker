Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence that focuses on building systems that can learn from data and improve their performance over time. It allows computers to identify patterns and make decisions without being explicitly programmed for every task.

Machine learning is widely used in various applications such as image recognition, speech recognition, recommendation systems, and fraud detection. For example, platforms like online shopping websites and streaming services use machine learning to suggest products or content based on user preferences.

There are different types of machine learning, including supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning. In supervised learning, the model is trained using labeled data, while in unsupervised learning, the model finds hidden patterns in data without labels.

Overall, machine learning plays a crucial role in modern technology and continues to grow rapidly in fields such as healthcare, finance, and automation.
